Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a birth chart?

Tap the "+" button on the Dashboard to create a new chart. Enter the birth date, birth time, and birth city. The app will calculate the complete Vedic birth chart (Kundali) instantly on your device.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. All astrological calculations are performed entirely on your device using the Swiss Ephemeris engine. The app works fully offline. An internet connection is only used for displaying advertisements.

How accurate are the calculations?

Karna AI Astrology uses the Swiss Ephemeris, the same astronomical calculation engine trusted by professional astrologers worldwide. It computes planetary positions with arc-second precision for accurate chart generation.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All your birth data and charts are stored locally on your device. Your data is never transmitted to any server. There is no user account, no cloud storage, and no server-side component. Deleting the app removes all data.

Why does the app show ads?

Karna AI Astrology is free to use. Advertisements help support ongoing development. Ads are served through Google AdMob and are non-personalized. No personal data is shared for advertising purposes.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual charts from within the app. To remove all data, simply uninstall the app from your device. Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling completely removes it.

What languages are supported?

The app is available in English and Hindi, including all astrology terminology in both languages. You can switch languages from the Settings screen.

What features does the app include?

The app includes: complete birth chart (Kundali) visualization, planetary positions and Nakshatra details, daily Panchang, marriage compatibility (36-point Gun Milan), Manglik Dosha analysis, career and health analysis, real-time planetary transits, Sade Sati alerts, personalized remedies (mantras, gemstones, charities), divisional charts, and 100+ yoga detection.
