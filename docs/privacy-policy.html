Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 23, 2026

Karna AI Astrology ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Karna AI Astrology mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

To generate your Vedic birth chart (Kundali), we collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Birth date (year, month, day)
  • Birth time (hour, minute)
  • Birth location (city selection from our database, which provides latitude and longitude coordinates)

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it (for example, when contacting us for support).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • On-device chart generation: To calculate and display your Vedic astrological birth chart, Dasha periods, Yoga analysis, Nakshatra details, and Panchanga data. All calculations are performed entirely on your device using the Swiss Ephemeris engine.
  • Local storage: To store your charts and calculation results on your device so you can access them offline.
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries or requests if you contact us.

3. Data Storage and Security

All your birth data and chart calculations are processed and stored entirely on your device. Your data is never transmitted to any external server. We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • All astrological calculations happen on-device using the Swiss Ephemeris — no internet connection is required.
  • Your birth data never leaves your phone.
  • Chart data is stored locally using the operating system's secure storage mechanisms (AsyncStorage).
  • There is no server-side component — the App works fully offline.

4. Advertising and Tracking

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect the following data for ad personalization and measurement:

  • Device Identifier (IDFA): Used for third-party advertising purposes. This data is not linked to your identity.

On iOS 14.5 and later, we request your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before any tracking occurs. You can decline tracking, and the App will still function normally with non-personalized ads.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties beyond what is necessary for ad serving through Google AdMob.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally on your device and is accessible to you at all times through the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete your charts and data from within the App's settings, or by uninstalling the App.
  • Ad tracking opt-out: You can decline ad tracking via the ATT prompt or change your preference in iOS Settings > Privacy > Tracking.

6. Children's Privacy

Karna AI Astrology is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Swiss Ephemeris: Open-source astronomical calculation library used on-device for chart generation. No data is sent to any external service.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
